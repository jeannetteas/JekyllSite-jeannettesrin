Jeannette Srinivasan (She/They) is a Queer BIPOC artist who’s strives to incorporate qualities of her middle school self: the extremely socially anxious teen who dreamt of telling stories through her acne prone face and body. They hope to continue their training at CalArts to one day de-class the theater scene and further open up spaces for marginalized groups of people.
